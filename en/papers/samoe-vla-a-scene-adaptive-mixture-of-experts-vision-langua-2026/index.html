Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Scene-adaptive Mixture-of-Experts routing conditioned on bird's-eye-view (BEV) features instead of token embeddings, enabling scenario-dependent expert weighting and merging.
  • Conditional Cross-Modal Causal Attention mechanism that integrates world state, linguistic intent, and action history into a unified causal reasoning process for temporally consistent reasoning.

Methodology

SAMoE-VLA is a Vision-Language-Action model that replaces token-level MoE routing with scene-level routing derived from BEV features to align expert specialization with driving contexts. It also introduces a Conditional Cross-Modal Causal Attention mechanism to unify world knowledge, perception, language, and action in a causal reasoning framework. The model is evaluated on the nuScenes open-loop planning dataset and the LangAuto closed-loop benchmark, comparing against prior VLA-based and world-model-based approaches.

Key Results

SAMoE-VLA achieves state-of-the-art performance on both nuScenes open-loop planning and LangAuto closed-loop benchmarks, outperforming prior VLA and world-model methods while using fewer parameters.
