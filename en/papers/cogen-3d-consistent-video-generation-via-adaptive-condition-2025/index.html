Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Replacing coarse 2D layout conditions (e.g., HD maps and bounding boxes) with fine-grained 3D conditions that capture the geometry of driving scenes to enhance spatial consistency.
  • Introducing a consistency adapter module to strengthen the robustness of the model to multi-condition control.

Methodology

CoGen is a spatial adaptive generation framework that first generates high-quality, controllable 3D conditions representing the geometry of driving scenes, replacing traditional 2D conditions. It then employs a consistency adapter module to improve robustness under multiple conditioning inputs, leveraging advances in 3D generation to produce multi-view videos with high 3D consistency.

Key Results

The method excels in preserving geometric fidelity and visual realism, offering a reliable video generation solution for autonomous driving.
