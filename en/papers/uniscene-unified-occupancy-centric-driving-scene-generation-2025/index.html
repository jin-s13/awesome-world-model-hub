Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • First unified framework for generating three key data forms (semantic occupancy, video, LiDAR) in driving scenes
  • Progressive generation process decomposing scene generation into two hierarchical steps: first generating semantic occupancy from a customized scene layout, then generating video and LiDAR conditioned on occupancy
  • Occupancy-centric meta representation that provides rich semantic and geometric information for subsequent generation stages
  • Two novel transfer strategies: Gaussian-based Joint Rendering for video generation and Prior-guided Sparse Modeling for LiDAR generation

Methodology

UniScene employs a progressive generation process that first generates semantic occupancy from a customized scene layout as a meta scene representation rich in both semantic and geometric information. Then, conditioned on this occupancy, it generates video and LiDAR data using two novel transfer strategies: Gaussian-based Joint Rendering and Prior-guided Sparse Modeling, respectively.

Key Results

UniScene outperforms previous state-of-the-art methods in occupancy, video, and LiDAR generation, and the generated data benefits downstream driving tasks.
