Methodology

DeformMaster is a physics-neural world model that combines structured physical rollout with a neural residual to compensate for unmodeled effects. It uses sparse hand motion as a distributed compliant actuator for hand-continuum interaction and represents material response with spatially varying constitutive experts. The model predicts physical evolution and renders high-fidelity 4D appearance from it, trained on real-world deformable-object videos.

Key Results

Experiments on real-world deformable-object sequences show DeformMaster outperforms state-of-the-art baselines in rolling out future dynamics and rendering dynamic appearance, while supporting novel action rollout, material-parameter variation, and dynamic novel-view synthesis.
