Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Identification of a backdoor vulnerability in world models rooted in the long-tailed ranking structure of imagined trajectories
  • Tail-aware ranking loss that focuses optimization on decision-critical trajectories
  • Dual gating mechanisms to stabilize optimization and regulate when and where the attack penalty is applied

Methodology

TRAP is a backdoor attack framework for world models that targets the ranking of imagined trajectories. It combines a tail-aware ranking loss to focus on decision-critical trajectories with dual gating mechanisms that stabilize optimization and regulate when and where the attack penalty is applied. Under trigger conditions, TRAP alters the relative ranking of imagined trajectories to redirect planning outcomes while largely maintaining the normal ranking structure on clean inputs.

Key Results

Experiments on DreamerV3 and TD-MPC2 across diverse tasks show that TRAP consistently induces sustained behavioral deviations and significant performance degradation.
